Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about business licensing

The processing time varies by license type and location. On average:

  • FSSAI Registration: 7-14 days
  • Trade License: 15-30 days
  • Shop & Establishment:3- 7 days
  • Professional License: 30-45 days

With GrowthLink's expedited services, we can often reduce these timeframes by 30-50%.

The required licenses depend on your business type, location, and scale. Generally:

  • All businesses need a Shop & Establishment License
  • Food businesses require FSSAI License
  • Retail stores need Trade License
  • Manufacturing units need Factory License
  • Professional services may require specific professional licenses

Our experts can provide a detailed assessment based on your specific business model.

Operating without mandatory licenses can lead to serious consequences:

  • Monetary penalties (ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹5,00,000)
  • Forced business closure
  • Legal prosecution
  • Imprisonment in severe cases
  • Inability to access business loans or government schemes

It's always advisable to ensure proper licensing before commencing operations.

Renewal frequencies vary by license type:

  • FSSAI License: 1-5 years depending on category
  • Trade License: Annually
  • Shop & Establishment: 3-5 years depending on state
  • Factory License: Annually
  • Professional License: 1-3 years depending on profession

GrowthLink offers renewal reminder services and can handle the entire renewal process for you.

Yes, you can apply for multiple licenses simultaneously, and in many cases, it's more efficient to do so. Our Professional and Enterprise packages are designed specifically for businesses needing multiple licenses.

Common documents required for most license applications include:

  • Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
  • Address proof (utility bills, property documents)
  • Business registration documents (Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed)
  • Property ownership/rental agreement
  • NOC from property owner (if rented)
  • Business plan or layout
  • Photographs of business premises
  • Bank account details

Specific licenses may require additional industry-specific documentation.
