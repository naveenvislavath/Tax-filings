Consequences of Non-Compliance

Understanding the risks of missing regulatory requirements

Financial Penalties

Significant monetary fines that increase with the duration of non-compliance

  • Late filing fees (₹100-₹1,000 per day)
  • Percentage-based penalties (up to 100% of tax due)
  • Compounding fees for regularization

Legal Consequences

Legal actions that can affect your business operations and reputation

  • Prosecution of directors/partners
  • Disqualification of directors
  • Legal notices and court proceedings

Business Restrictions

Operational limitations that can hinder your business growth

  • Frozen bank accounts
  • Restricted business transactions
  • Inability to secure loans or investments

Reputation Damage

Long-term impact on your brand image and stakeholder trust

  • Loss of customer trust
  • Strained vendor/supplier relationships
  • Negative impact on business valuation
