License Requirements

Everything you need to know about obtaining your food and trade licenses

Eligibility Criteria

FSSAI Registration

  • Basic Registration: Annual turnover up to ₹12 lakhs
  • State License: Annual turnover between ₹12 lakhs to ₹20 crores
  • Central License: Annual turnover exceeding ₹20 crores or operating in multiple states
  • Food business must comply with safety and hygiene standards

Trade License

  • Business must be operating within municipal corporation limits
  • Premises must comply with local zoning regulations
  • Business activities must not violate any local laws or regulations
  • Applicant must be the owner or have authorization from the owner

Shop Establishment License

  • Any commercial establishment with employees
  • Business must comply with working hours and conditions as per the Act
  • Premises must meet safety and sanitation requirements
  • Registration required within 30 days of business commencement

Document Requirements

FSSAI Registration

  • Identity proof of business owner/partners (Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/PAN)
  • Business registration documents (Partnership deed/Certificate of Incorporation)
  • Proof of business premises (Rent agreement/Ownership deed)
  • NOC from local municipality/panchayat
  • List of food products manufactured/processed/sold
  • Water analysis report (for manufacturing units)

Trade License

  • Application form with business details
  • Identity proof of applicant
  • Address proof of business premises
  • Property tax receipt
  • NOC from property owner (if rented)
  • Partnership deed/MOA/AOA (for companies)

Shop Establishment License

  • Application form with business details
  • Identity proof of employer/business owner
  • Address proof of business premises
  • Rent agreement/Ownership deed
  • List of employees with details
  • Business registration documents

Renewal Procedures

FSSAI License Renewal

  • Basic Registration: Valid for 5 years, renewal application to be filed 30 days before expiry
  • State/Central License: Valid for 1-5 years, renewal application to be filed 30 days before expiry
  • Late renewal attracts penalty of ₹100 per day up to 90 days
  • Beyond 90 days, fresh application is required

Trade License Renewal

  • Typically valid for 1 financial year (April to March)
  • Renewal application to be filed 1 month before expiry
  • Late renewal attracts penalty as per municipal rules
  • Required documents: Existing license, application form, fee payment receipt

Shop Establishment License Renewal

  • Validity varies by state (1-5 years)
  • Renewal application to be filed 30 days before expiry
  • Required documents: Existing license, renewal application, updated employee details
  • Late renewal may require fresh application in some states

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about food and trade licensing

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) registration is a mandatory license for all food businesses in India. It ensures that your food business complies with the safety and hygiene standards set by the Food Safety and Standards Act, 2006. Operating without an FSSAI license can lead to penalties up to ₹5 lakhs, business closure, and legal action.

The processing time for FSSAI licenses varies based on the type of registration:
- Basic Registration: 7-10 working days
- State License: 15-20 working days
- Central License: 25-30 working days
These timelines may vary based on document completeness and authority workload. With GrowthLink's services, we ensure faster processing by ensuring error-free applications and regular follow-ups.

A Trade License is issued by the municipal corporation and permits you to run a specific trade or business within city limits. It focuses on the nature of business activities.

A Shop Establishment License is governed by the Shop and Establishment Act and regulates the working conditions of employees in commercial establishments. It focuses on labor compliance, working hours, and employee welfare.

Most businesses require both licenses to operate legally.

Yes, you can apply for these licenses yourself through the respective government portals. However, the process can be complex and time-consuming due to specific documentation requirements, changing regulations, and follow-up procedures. Professional services like GrowthLink ensure error-free applications, faster processing, and handle all follow-ups with authorities, saving you time and reducing the risk of application rejection.

Operating without required licenses can lead to:
- Monetary penalties (up to ₹5 lakhs for FSSAI violations)
- Forced business closure
- Legal proceedings against business owners
- Ineligibility for business loans and government schemes
- Damage to business reputation
- Difficulty in securing contracts with organized retailers or corporate clients

Yes, all these licenses require periodic renewal:
- FSSAI Basic Registration: Every 5 years
- FSSAI State/Central License: Every 1-5 years (as opted)
- Trade License: Annually (typically financial year based)
- Shop Establishment License: Varies by state (1-5 years)

Operating with an expired license is considered illegal. GrowthLink offers renewal management services with timely reminders to ensure continuous compliance.

Government fees vary based on license type and business size:
- FSSAI Basic Registration: ₹100 to ₹500
- FSSAI State License: ₹2,000 to ₹5,000
- FSSAI Central License: ₹7,500 to ₹15,000
- Trade License: ₹500 to ₹5,000 (varies by municipality and business type)
- Shop Establishment License: ₹500 to ₹3,000 (varies by state and employee count)

These fees are separate from our service charges and are paid directly to government authorities.
